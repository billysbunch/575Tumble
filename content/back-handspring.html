BACK HANDSPRING

This 45-minute class is the next step for students who have learned a back-handspring without assistance on the floor.
These athletes will work on more advanced tumbling skills including: back tucks, back layouts, and multiple handsprings
forwards and backwards. An emphasis will also be placed on executing a tumbling series including: 1) round-off back handspring, 2)
round-of back handspring back tuck, and 3) round-off back handspring back layout( five consistent back handspring have
to be accomplished befor teaching a back tuck).
