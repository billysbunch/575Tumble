BEGINNER

This 45 minute class is for the beginner of different ages. Students are taught skills on each of the four main tumbling events: bars, balance beam, trampoline, and floor.
Tumblers warm-up for the first 15-minutes of each class, incorporating cardio, flexibility, and strength into their warm-up. The remainder of the class time is
equally spent on the mechanics of rolling, backbends, cart wheels. Jumping on the trampoline is always a fun activity.
