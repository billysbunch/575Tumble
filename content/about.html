575 TUMBLING

WHERE ROYALTY TRAINS

Hello, I am LaToya Jackson- Hooks owner of 575 Tumbling.

I have always wanted to own my own gym. In 2016 my dream finally became a reality.
I was given the opportunity and ran with it. I have worked in the cheer world since 2000.
Former NCA Staffer for 5 years, UCA for 2 years. Cheered in college at UTA GO MAVS,
where I was captain for 2 years.

After college I began working at Gymkix where I was fortunate to be an All-Star Coach. We
had many amazing seasons, and won a great deal of national championships. Working their is
where I learned the most, and my skills set grew the most. I have a huge passion for cheer,
dance and tumbling and want to be able to shared my God given talent with others.
